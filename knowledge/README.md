# Knowledge Base

Drop files here to give the business advisor deeper context about your situation. The more specific data you provide, the sharper the advice.

## What to Add

- **Demographics** — census data, population stats, income levels for your area
- **Lease/rent documents** — terms, costs, square footage, duration
- **Location photos** — storefront, neighborhood, foot traffic areas
- **Competitor info** — screenshots, menus, price lists, reviews of nearby businesses
- **Market research** — industry reports, local business surveys
- **Zoning/licensing** — municipal regulations, permitted business types
- **Financial data** — your budget breakdown, existing revenue numbers

## Organization

For a few files, just drop them here. If you have many, organize into subfolders:

```
knowledge/
  demographics/
  competitors/
  location/
  financials/
```

## How It's Used

When you ask for advice, the advisor will check this directory and incorporate any relevant data into the analysis — giving you recommendations grounded in your actual situation rather than general knowledge.
