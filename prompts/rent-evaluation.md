# Rent & Location Evaluation

Use this when you're considering a specific space and want to know if the rent is worth it.

## Template

> I'm looking at a **[size]** space in **[location/neighborhood]**. The rent is **[amount/month]**. The lease terms are **[duration, deposit, conditions]**. I want to use it for **[business type]**. Is this a good deal? What should I watch out for?

## Examples

> There's a 40 sqm shop on the main street in my town, rent is 2,000 TND/month with a 3-year lease and 6 months deposit upfront. I want to open a phone accessories and repair shop. The street has good foot traffic but there are already 2 phone shops within 200m. Is this rent reasonable? Should I negotiate?

> I found a 120 sqm space in an industrial zone outside Rabat for 5,000 MAD/month. I want to use it as a small food production kitchen (catering + delivery). The location is far from customers but rent is cheap. Is the trade-off worth it?

> A friend is offering me his restaurant space because he's closing down. 80 sqm, fully equipped kitchen, downtown location. He wants 15,000 MAD/month which is above market rate but the equipment alone is worth a lot. Should I take it or find a cheaper empty space and equip it myself?
