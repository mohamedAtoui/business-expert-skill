# New Business Idea

Use this when you're looking for what business to start in a specific area.

## Template

> I'm thinking about starting a business in **[city/neighborhood]**. My budget is around **[amount]**. My background is in **[your skills/experience]**. The area is **[describe: residential, commercial, near university, etc.]** with mostly **[demographics: families, students, workers, etc.]**. What would you recommend?

## Examples

> My brother has a space available in a small town of 15,000 people in southern Tunisia. The rent is 800 TND/month. The area has a lot of families and some students. Budget is around 30,000 TND. He's good with his hands and has worked in retail. What business would work best here?

> I found a commercial space on a busy street in Casablanca near a university campus. 50 sqm, rent is 8,000 MAD/month. I have about 200,000 MAD to invest. I have experience in food service. What should I open?

> There's an empty storefront in a suburban neighborhood in Lyon, France. Mostly families with young children, nearest shops are 2km away. I have €40,000 and no specific business experience but I'm a fast learner. What are my best options?
