# Market Analysis

Use this when you want to understand whether a specific business type can work in an area.

## Template

> Analyze the market opportunity for a **[business type]** in **[location]**. The existing competition includes **[known competitors]**. The target customers would be **[description]**. The area has **[relevant characteristics]**.

## Examples

> Is there room for a coworking space in a city of 80,000 people in Morocco? There's one existing coworking space that's always full and charges 1,500 MAD/month. The city has a growing number of freelancers and remote workers. I have a 200 sqm space available.

> I want to understand if a car wash business makes sense near the highway exit in my town. There are 2 existing ones but they're both basic hand-wash operations. The area gets heavy traffic. Is there room for an automated car wash?

> My neighborhood in Istanbul is getting a new metro station next year. Right now it's mostly residential with few shops. What businesses typically thrive when a new transit hub opens? I want to be early.
