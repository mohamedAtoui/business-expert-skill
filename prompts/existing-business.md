# Existing Business Problems

Use this when you have a running business and need help diagnosing or fixing issues.

## Template

> I run a **[type of business]** in **[location]**. We've been open for **[duration]**. Our main challenge is **[describe the problem]**. Monthly revenue is around **[amount]** and costs are about **[amount]**. What should I change?

## Examples

> I run a small grocery store in a residential area in Algiers. Been open 2 years. Revenue is around 400,000 DZD/month but after rent, stock, and one employee, I barely break even. Foot traffic has dropped since a supermarket opened 500m away. What can I do?

> My barbershop in Marseille has been open 6 months. I get about 8 clients/day but I need at least 15 to cover my costs. I'm on a side street with no visibility from the main road. How do I get more customers without spending a lot on marketing?

> I have a clothing store that does well in winter but dies in summer. Revenue drops 60% from June to September. I can't afford to keep paying full rent during dead months. What are my options?
