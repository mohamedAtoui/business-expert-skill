# Research Plan & Search Templates

## Search Strategy by Category

### A. Location Research (3-4 searches)
Web search queries:
- "[address/neighborhood] businesses"
- "[city/town] demographics population income"
- "[neighborhood] development plans [year]"

Browser tools (if available):
- Navigate to Google Maps, search the address
- Note surrounding businesses, ratings, review counts
- If browser tools fail, fall back to WebSearch

### B. Competitor Analysis (4-5 searches)
- "[business type] near [location]" — for each candidate type
- For each competitor found: name, distance, rating, review count, price range
- Review themes: what customers praise and complain about
- "[business type] closed [city]" — find failures and learn why

### C. Demand Signals (3-4 searches)
- Google Trends: "[business category] [region/country]"
- Local forums: "[city] what's missing" or "[neighborhood] need more"
- Local news: "[city] new businesses [current year]"
- Social media: Facebook groups, Reddit for the area

### D. Regulatory Research (2-3 searches)
- "[country/city] [business type] license requirements"
- "[country] commercial permit cost timeline"
- Zoning regulations for the specific area

### E. Financial Benchmarks (2-3 searches)
- "[business type] average revenue [country]"
- "[business type] profit margins [region]"
- "[business type] startup costs [country]"

### F. Comparative Intelligence (2-3 searches)
This is what makes the skill unique. For each top candidate:
- "[business type] success story [comparable city size]"
- "[business type] failed why [region]"
- Look for patterns: what conditions made similar businesses succeed or fail in comparable markets

## Language Strategy
| Region | Search Languages | Query Style |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| MENA / North Africa | Arabic + French | Construct natively, don't translate |
| Francophone Africa | French + local language | Native construction |
| Latin America | Spanish/Portuguese | Native construction |
| Europe | Local language + English | Both |
| English-speaking | English | Standard |
| East/Southeast Asia | Local language + English | Both |

## Output Files
Save each category to its own file in `state/research/`:
- `location-analysis.md` — Surroundings, accessibility, traffic patterns
- `competitors.md` — Every competitor with ratings, pricing, review themes
- `demographics.md` — Population, income, age, growth indicators
- `demand-signals.md` — Trends, community needs, growth signals
- `regulations.md` — Licensing, permits, costs, timelines
- `research-summary.md` — Key findings, surprises, remaining gaps
